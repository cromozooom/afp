THE MAGIC OF THE SQUARE

AMAZING FRAMEABLE POSTCARDS
Each postcard is square shaped.
The square is a perfect balanced and a very stable shape. The square format lends itself to a simple approach. And by simplifying, the composition intensifies the subject.

FRAMEABLE POSTCARDS

The square format provides the perfect balanced frame. We developed a simple, yet awesome frame, available in different colors that suit each postcard, giving you the possibility to easily frame your favorite. The framed postcards became exquisite objects to decorate your room or office.

THE STORY BEHIND

There is an interesting story behind every great image. Following the QR code on the back of the Amazing Frameable Postcards, you will trace the fascinating story behind the image printed on the postcards, gathered all on the blog.
